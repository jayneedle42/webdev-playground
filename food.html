Canasta de Maiz con Atasco

  • Jiffy Cornbread Mix (8.5 oz)
  • 3 Egg Yolks
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 1 lb of Fresh Fruit (Apples/Pineapple)
  • 1 ¼ cup water
  • ½ teaspoon Unflavored Gelatin
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice or ½ tbsp vinegar
  • Cinnamon to taste

1. Simmer fruit and ¾ cup water in deep pan for about 15 minutes

2. Add sugar and simmer on lower setting for about 25 minutes

3. Dissolve gelatin in ½ cup water in microwave

4. Add acidity, cinnamon, gelatin and simmer for about 5 minutes

5. Cool in fridge.

6. Mix dry ingredients in bowl.

7. Separate into 5 or 6 equal portions

8. Form into bottom of greased pan

9. Fill with jam

10. Bake at 375 for 10-13 minutes
